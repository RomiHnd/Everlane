Women's Dresses FAQs

How do I style a casual dress?

How do I style a casual dress?

Whether for farmer’s markets, running errands, or coffee dates, we love to style our casual dresses with low-key flats like sneakers, ballet pumps, or Mary Janes. Adding layers with an oversized shirt, denim jacket, chore coat, or even a chunky relaxed sweater keeps things versatile and different every time.

What dresses are best to wear in the office?

What dresses are best to wear in the office?

Depending on your work situation—formal or more casual, you’ll want office dresses that are easy to wear, rotate, and repeat—while looking different every time. Comfortable to wear all day—and night if needed, they also need to look the part too. Wrap dresses, shirt dresses, midi or maxi dresses, all fit the brief. It’s also down to the styling too. Even your favorite T-shirt or tank dresses will work overtime paired with a blazer and heels.

What are the best types of dresses that you can wear from

day to night?

What are the best types of dresses that you can wear from day to night?

RSVP yes to all events from co-working to cocktails, in versatile dresses you can easily take from day to night (and night to day—we won’t judge). Whether mini, midi, or maxi dresses—and, of course you can’t go wrong in black dresses. Wear what feels most comfortable to you and then style accordingly. Bring extra accessories and shoes with you, to transition your dress for after-hours.
